About

Hello! My name is David O'Donohue, and I am a registered psychologist and clinical psychology registrar. I am currently accepting new clients (note that I do not provide assessment services, and am not accepting new clients under the age of 18). New and existing clients can make appointments either online through the booking page, or through the Halaxy online portal. To make an inquiry, or for assistance in booking an appointment, please contact me via email at pendulumpsychology@gmail.com.

I believe that mental health can be viewed in terms of internal conflict, and that avoiding or suppressing distressing emotions or thinking patterns can lead to pendulumical outcomes. I think it's important to realise that there are no strictly positive or negative emotions or thinking patterns, and to understand how each can be useful or not useful depending on the context. By understanding the positive aspects of an emotion or way of thinking, we can respond to it differently. By allowing all aspects of ourselves to be present and honoured, we can create a more harmonious and balanced internal landscape.

Services

I offer psychological therapy to adult clients (clients aged 18 and over).
I do not offer psychological assessments. I also do not offer therapeutic services to clients under the age of 18.
