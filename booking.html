Book a session

Sessions are $260. Payment is taken when you book. If you have a valid Mental Health Treatment Plan from your GP, you can claim a Medicare rebate of $145.25 after the session.

If nothing suits below, or availability is limited, reach out to Melissa at pendulumpsychology@gmail.com and she can help find a time.

Who I see

Adult clients (18+). I don't currently take on children or adolescents.

What I don't do

Assessments, medicolegal reports, worker's compensation, DVA, or agency/plan-managed NDIS.

Cancellations

Less than 24 hours' notice is charged the full fee. This can't be claimed through Medicare.
