Avatar: The Last Airbender Quiz



Just as benders in the Avatar universe connect with their natural element, therapists and clients often have innate therapeutic styles that reflect deeper patterns in how they approach growth and healing.


Water represents deep emotional attunement and flowing with what emerges. Earth embodies steadiness, practical wisdom, and building sustainable change. Fire captures the transformative power of active engagement. Air reflects the clarity that comes from understanding patterns and meaning.


Understanding your therapeutic element may help you:

  • Recognise your natural strengths and preferences in therapy
  • Find approaches that resonate with your style
  • Better understand how you engage with therapeutic change
  • Appreciate different pathways to growth and healing
